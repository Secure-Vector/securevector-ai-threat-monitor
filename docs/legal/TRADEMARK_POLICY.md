## Forks and Derivative Works

The Apache 2.0 license **permits you to fork our code**, but **NOT to use our trademarks** on your fork.

### If You Fork Our Code:

**✅ DO:**
- Remove or replace all SecureVector trademarks
- Choose a clearly different name
- Clearly state it's a fork: "MyProduct (forked from SecureVector)"
- Comply with Apache 2.0 attribution requirements

**❌ DON'T:**
- Use "SecureVector" in your product name
- Use our logos or visual identity
- Imply you are SecureVector or affiliated with us

**Examples:**

| ❌ Not Allowed | ✅ Allowed |
|---------------|-----------|
| SecureVector Community | ThreatGuard (based on SecureVector) |
| SecureVector Plus | AIShield (forked from SecureVector) |
| SecureVector Enterprise | SafetyMonitor (using SecureVector core) |
